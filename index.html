ABOUT SQUAD V

Squad V is a grassroots football academy based in Hayes, dedicated to developing young talent and fostering a competitive spirit within our community.

We believe in the power of football to build character, confidence, and skills in our players.

Our coaching team brings years of experience in youth football development. We focus on technical skill development, tactical awareness, and building a strong team culture.

Whether you're a beginner or aiming to compete at a higher level, Squad V provides the training, support, and community to succeed.
